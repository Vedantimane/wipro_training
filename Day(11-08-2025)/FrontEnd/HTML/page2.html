Anime Character: Mikasa Ackerman

Mikasa Ackerman is one of the main characters in the anime Attack on Titan. She is a skilled soldier, known for her exceptional combat abilities and loyalty to her friends.

  • Anime: Attack on Titan
  • Affiliation: Survey Corps
  • Abilities: Exceptional swordsmanship, agility, and strength
  • Personality: Brave, protective, determined
